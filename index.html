DRAGON EGG HUNT

Instructions for Dragon Egg hunting


You will be prompted with an amount of gold you must obtain.

There are four types of Dragon Eggs to cho0se from. Clicking on a Dragon Egg will allot you a specific amount of gold.

In order to win the hunt, your total must match the prompted amount for the hunting session. If you go over the prompted amount you lose the game.

The value of each of the Dragon Eggs is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time a new game starts, the value of the Dragon Eggs change.
